Some day you'll be able to buy a domain right from this page. Until then, you can buy your own domain and point it to your 42pag.es website.

First, buy a domain on a site like Gandi or Namecheap.

Then, create a CNAME record from www.your-site.com to 42pag.es.

(You'll also want to set up your naked domain (you-site.com, without the www) to redirect to www.your-site.com)

Then, enter your custom domain here:

{{ settings | safe }}

Then, after about an hour, you'll be able to access your website from your new domain.

Note that on your custom domain, you won't see any edit buttons. To edit, access the website at its old URL, {{ site_name }}.42pag.es.
